The Workflow

Four Steps.
Extraordinary Results.

We've refined our process into a simple, transparent workflow that keeps you informed at every stage — while we do the heavy creative lifting.

01
Discovery

Brief & Creative Direction

Every project starts with a conversation. We take the time to understand your brand, goals, audience, and aesthetic preferences. You fill out a simple creative brief, and we follow up with a call to dive deeper into your vision. This stage ensures that every creative decision we make is intentional and aligned with what you need.

Creative Brief Brand Audit Moodboarding Goal Setting
02
Concepting

AI-Assisted Concept Development

Using the insights from your brief, our team crafts prompt strategies across leading AI platforms. We run hundreds of generations, curate the strongest outputs, and refine them with our proprietary workflows. You receive a concept presentation with multiple distinct directions — not just one path, but real creative choices.

Midjourney Stable Diffusion Flux Kling Runway
03
Refinement

Feedback, Iteration & Polish

You review the concepts and share feedback. We take that input and iterate — tightening compositions, refining color grading, adjusting tone, and enhancing detail. All images and video outputs go through a manual QA process. We never deliver raw AI outputs; every piece is intentionally crafted and post-processed by our creative team.

Up to 3 Revision Rounds Photoshop Retouching Color Grading Upscaling
04
Delivery

Final Files & Handoff

Once approved, we package all final files in the formats you need — high-res images, web-optimized exports, video files — and deliver them via a clean, organized shared drive. You also receive a usage guide and, for brand projects, a visual style reference document so your team can maintain consistency going forward.

Hi-Res PNG / JPG MP4 / MOV Web Optimized Style Reference
